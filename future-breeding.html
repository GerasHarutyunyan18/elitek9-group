Breeding Program

Our world-class studs and dams available for your breeding goals.

Our Studs (Males)

Titan

German Shepherd (Czech Line)

Titan is a powerhouse of a dog with extreme drives and a crushing grip. He is social with family but serious in protection work. His progeny inherit his stability, bone structure, and clear head. Proven producer of police and sport candidates.

IGP 3 KKL 1 HD/ED Normal

Bolt

Belgian Malinois (NVBK Line)

Bolt brings lightning-fast speed and unshakeable nerves to the table. He is currently competing in Ringsport at the highest level. He adds drive, athleticism, and environmental stability to any breeding program.

Ring Sport III FCI Working EPI Negative

Our Dams (Females)

Xena

German Shepherd (West German)

Xena is the epitome of the female German Shepherd. She is substantial in size yet feminine in expression. She is a devoted mother and a fierce personal protector. She produces puppies with excellent pigment and balanced drives.

IGP 1 PPD Level 2 OFA Good

Luna

Belgian Malinois (KNPV Line)

Luna is a serious working female with deep hunt drive. She is currently an active narcotics detection dog. We breed her to produce serious working prospects for law enforcement and military applications.

NNDDA Certified Active K9 OFA Excellent
