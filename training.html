Family Protection Training

Real security. Real control. Zero aggression issues.

Our protection training is defense-based, not aggression-based. Your dog will learn to recognize threats and act only when necessary, while remaining a gentle family pet 99% of the time.

Key Skills:

  • Threat recognition
  • Bark and hold (alerting on command)
  • Controlled apprehension (only if necessary)
  • Immediate "Out" (release) command
